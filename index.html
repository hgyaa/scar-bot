what scar can do

Everything your server needs

From moderation to economy to auto-welcomes — scar handles it all so you can focus on your community.

🛡️
Advanced Moderation
Ban, kick, timeout, warn, jail, and more — all with DM notifications, custom messages, and full audit logs. Includes word filter, link filter, lockdowns, and channel nuke tools.
🔒
AntiNuke Protection
Protect your server from raiders with configurable thresholds for bans, kicks, channel deletions, role changes, webhook spam, and bot adds. Auto-punish with ban, kick, or role strip.
👋
Auto Welcomer
Greet new members with custom embed messages across up to 4 channels. Full variable support including mention, username, and server name.
💰
Economy System
Full coin economy with daily rewards, coin-flip gambling, leaderboards, and member-to-member transfers.
🤖
Autoresponder
Set up keyword triggers that auto-reply with custom messages. Great for FAQs and fun server reactions.
📊
Server Logging
Log message edits, deletions, joins, leaves, bans, role changes, voice activity and more to a dedicated channel.
🏷️
Vanity Roles
Auto-assign roles when members add your server's vanity text to their Discord status. Customisable reward roles and thank-you messages.
🎭
Roleplay
Slash commands for interactive GIF-based roleplay actions — hug, kiss, cuddle, punch, boop, and more. Powered by waifu.pics.
🔔
Bump Reminder
Automatically remind members to bump your server on Disboard every 2 hours. Optional channel autolock and custom thank-you messages for bumpers.
🎤
Join-to-Create
Temporary voice channels that auto-create when a member joins and delete when empty. Members get a full control panel to lock, rename, and limit their channel.
🧼
Swear Jar
Track swear word usage per member with a customisable word list and per-user counts. Great for keeping friendly competition in your community.
✨
Embed Builder
Create and send rich embeds with an interactive button-based editor — full colour, field, and live preview support.
😄
Custom Emojis
Set your own server-specific emojis for success, failure, enabled, and disabled states. Every response feels native to your server's vibe.
🎵
Last.fm Integration
Link your Last.fm account and share what you're currently listening to directly in Discord with a verified profile and recent track display.
⚡
Message React & Fake Permissions
Auto-react to messages by user or trigger word across any channel. Pair it with fake permissions to grant specific command access without changing Discord roles — total flexibility for how you run your server.
